This website is created to help students to understand Japanese culture. We will provide informations about Japanese culture. After viewing our website, you can take a quiz and test your understanding of Japanese culture.

Japan is a sovereign island nation in East Asia. Located in the Pacific Ocean, it lies off the eastern coast of the Asian mainland, and stretches from the Sea of Okhotsk in the north to the East China Sea and Taiwan in the southwest.

Hardly a day goes by that you don't hear something about Japan, whether the subject is trade, travel, cuisine, the arts, or Japanese imports ranging from Sony and Toyota to karaoke and anime.

Japanese Food

The traditional cuisine of Japan is based on rice with miso soup and other dishes; there is an emphasis on seasonal ingredients. Side dishes often consist of fish, pickled vegetables, and vegetables cooked in broth. ... Apart from rice, staples include noodles, such as soba and udon.

Japanese Festival

Japanese festivals are traditional festive occasions. Some festivals have their roots in Chinese festivals centuries ago, but have undergone great changes as they mixed with local customs.

Japanese Clothing

Clothing in Japan evolved along a unique and fascinating course. It was influenced by factors such as battle and the weather. It was also greatly influenced by the design of Japanese homes. While the traditional ethnic garments of Japan are still in use, they are mainly worn for ceremonies and special events, funerals, coming-of-age ceremonies , and festivals.
